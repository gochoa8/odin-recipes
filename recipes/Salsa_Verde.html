Salsa Verde (green sauce)

Mexican style green sauce

Impress your friends with this delicious mexican style green sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 avocado
  • 1 cup packed parsley and cilantro leaves (combined)
  • 1 jalapeno, ribs and seeds removed
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • juice of one lime or two
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup pistachios

Steps

  1. Pulse all ingredients,except pistachios, in a food processor until incorporated.
  2. Add pistachios and pulse until mostly smooth (depends on what consistency you want).
